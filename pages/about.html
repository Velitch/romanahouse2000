October 2018 - May 2022

Concervatorio Santa Cecilia - AV Technician

In October 2018 I was nominated Sound and Visual Technician at the Conservatoire Santa Cecilia, a role that has evolved to the position of Senior and has been my profession until May 2022. In the last three years I have been the main AV technician working in the building, so I had all the responsibilities within the sound department. Here I had the opportunity to work with talented artists. These sessions required a deep understanding of acoustics, microphone placement, and mixing techniques, and I was able to bring my technical knowledge and artistic vision to the table to help the artists achieve their desired sound.

December 2018 - October 2020

Centro Ricerche Musicali - Sound & Production Techncician

The CRM - Centro Ricerche Musicali is a centre where artists, musicians, scientists, architects and researchers work together. With them I’ve worked for festivals, concerts and sound art installations; from building the equipment till the performance, I was taking care of all the sound aspects. The best aspect of this role was that they were considering me as a creative more than a technician, so I had the possibility to grow really close to artistic management.

I was the main assistant of the laboratories and personal assistant of the Art Director. Duties were going from soldering circuits, to writing sound algorithms, to fit up festivals, concerts and sound art installations around Italy. With the CRM I've learned how to work in a small team, where all the technical equipment is built specially for the event.

I've been lucky to grow my skills in a multidisciplinary context where arts, sciences and technologies are merged all in one.

February 2022 - May 2022

Università Roma Tre - Lecturer of Digital Music Technologies

I’m a proficient talker, able to jump from technical to non-technical conversations without losing the goal. This skill and my passion for theory helped me to be directly called by the University of Roma Tre for the position of Lecturer of Music technologies. The aim of the laboratory is to provide the student with basic skills concerning electroacoustic and multimedia technologies applied to music. In particular, the students will be involved in the realisation of compositional and performative projects which foresee a practical use of the acquired technical specifications. Furthermore, the laboratory intends to offer the possibility of acquiring basic knowledge in the field of music theory. The educational material used during the course comes from my personal notes, available on my website.
